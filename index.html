A note from the owners

A neighbourhood café happens to be inside a small hotel.

Isabella Suites opened with seventeen rooms upstairs and a problem downstairs: nowhere good to drink a coffee. So we built the café we wanted to walk into on a Tuesday morning. Beans from a roaster we actually like, and a pastry case we don't have to apologise for.

By lunchtime the bagels and panini come out. By five the lights drop, the Aperols come up, and the room shifts into an aperitivo bar — burrata, mortadella, a glass of Pinot Grigio, the works.

We're not trying to be the loudest café in Malta. We'd rather be your favourite one.

— Raj & the Isabella team
