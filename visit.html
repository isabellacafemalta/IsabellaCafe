Practical notes

A few things worth knowing before you come.

Getting hereBy foot, bus or car

Set in Gżira close to the marina, the Ta' Xbiex waterfront and Manoel Island bridge. Plenty of buses stop on Triq Manwel Dimech — routes 13, 16 and 222 all run nearby. On-street parking in Gżira is busy; the public car park at Manoel Island is your safest bet.

Work-friendlyWi-Fi, plugs & quiet

Free Wi-Fi, plugs on most tables and a calm room outside of the morning rush. Bring your laptop, no minimum spend — though we'd appreciate it if you didn't camp on a table for six hours with one espresso.

Bookings & groupsFor breakfast & aperitivo

Walk-ins are always welcome. For weekend breakfasts and groups of 6+, give us a call or drop us a WhatsApp the day before. We can put two tables together and pre-order a platter if you let us know.
